Hot Butered Rum

Steps

1. 1 lb brown sugar

2. 1 lb butter

3. 1 lb vanilla ice cream

4. 2 tbsp cinnamon

5. 1/2 tbsp tea cloves

6. 1/2 tbsp nutmeg

Let all ingredients soften at room temperature.
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.Refreeze in tight container.
To serve, mix 1/4 cup of mix with 1 shot of rum.
Fill mug with boiling water. Stir and enjoy.

Frozen Brandy Slush

Steps

1. 2 cups sugar

2. 7 cups water

3. bring 7 cups of water to a boil

4. 2 cups water, 4 black tea bags- bring to a boil

5. 12 oz frozen lemonade

6. 12 oz frozen orange juice

7. 2 cups Brandy

Mix all together in a gallon ice cream bucket and put into freezer until frozen slush.
Fill sherbert glass 2/3 full of slush. Fill glass with 7up or sprite.
Be sure water mixtures cool completely before putting them together.
