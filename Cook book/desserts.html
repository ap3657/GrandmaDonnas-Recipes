Brownies

Hershey Syrup Brownies

Steps

1. 1 cup sugar

2. 1/2 cup oleo

3. 4 eggs

4. 1 can hershey syrup

5. 1 tsp vanilla

6. 1 cup plus 2 tbsp flour

Mix ingredients in greased 9x13 pan. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Frost while warm.

Frosting

1. 1 cup sugar

2. 4 tbsp milk

3. 4 tbsp oleo

Boil for 30 seconds, add 1/2 chocolate chips, stir until melted. Spread on brownies.

Bars

Lemon Bars

Steps

1. 2 cups flour

2. 1 cup oleo

3. 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Press into 9x13 pan. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Beat together 4 eggs, 2 cups sugar, 1/2 cup lemon juice.
Sift 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 tbsp baking powder, stir into egg mixture.
Pour over oven crust and bake an additional 25 minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Cookies

Salted Peanut Cookie

Steps

1. 1 cup brown sugar

2. 1 cup shortening (not butter)

3. 1 cup ground salted peanuts

4. 1 cup oatmeal

5. 1 tsp. baking powder

6. 1 cup white sugar

7. 2 eggs

8. 1 cup crushed corn flakes

9. 2 cups flour

10. 1 tsp vanilla

Mix, roll into balls and roll in sugar. Bake at 350.

Cakes

Carrot Cake

Steps

1. 3 cups grated carrots

2. 1 cup walnuts

3. 1/2 cup oil

4. 4 eggs

5. 2 cups flour

6. 1 tsp. cinnamon

7. 1 tsp. salt

8. 2 tbsp. baking soda

9. 1 tsp. vanilla

10. 1/2 cup sugar

Mix all together, beat well. Bake at 350 in 9x13 greased pan for 40 minutes,
or until knife inserted comes out clean.

Cream Cheese Frosting

1. 8 oz cream cheese

2. 1 tsp. vanilla

3. 1/2 cup margarine

4. 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Mix cream cheese, vanilla, add margarine. Add powdered sugar, mix well. Frost cake.

Pies

Oh So Good Raisin Pie

Steps

1. 1 cup sugar

2. 1 tbsp. butter

3. 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

4. 1/4 tsp. cloves

5. pinch of salt

6. 2 small tbsp. vinegar

7. 1/2 cup raisins

8. 2 eggs

Stir all together, pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake at 350 until knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Candies

Cherry Mash Candy

Steps

1. 12 regular sized marshmallows

2. 2 cups sugar

3. 1/2 cup butter

4. 2/3 cup evaporated milk

5. 1 lb salted peanuits, ground fine

6. 1 pkg. milk chocolate chips

7. 1/8 tsp. salt

8. 1 pkg. cherry chips

9. 3/4 cup peanut butter

In large saucepan, put marshmallows, sugar, butter, evaporated milk, and salt.
Stir on medium heat until smooth. Boil 5 minutes. Keep stirring,
take off heat and add cherry chips until melted. Pour into buttered 9x13 pan.
Chill combined peanut butter and chocolate chips over low heat until melted smooth.
Add finely chopped peanuts. Pour over cherry layer and chill.

Sauce
