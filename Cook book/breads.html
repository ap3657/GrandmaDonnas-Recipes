White Bread Loaf

Steps

1. 3 cups warm water

2. 1 tsp. sugar

3. 2 tsp. yeast

4. Stir all ingredients until dissolved.

5. Beat in 4 cups flour. Beat well.

6. Add 2 cups lukewarm milk

7. 6 tsp. shortening

8. 2 tsp. salt

Stir together well, add enough flour (1 cup at a time) to make a soft dough that you can knead.
Turn onto board and knead until smoothe and elastic, about 100 times.
Allow to rise until double in size. Punch down. Form into loaves. Let rise until double.
Bake at 350, 50 minutes or until browned. Should make 4-6 loaves depending on bread pan size.
