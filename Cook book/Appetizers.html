Apple Brickle Dip

Steps

1. 1 pkg. (8 oz) cream cheese, softened

2. 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

3. 1/4 cup sugar

4. 1 tsp. vanilla extract

5. 1 pkg. english toffee chips

6. 3 medium tart apples, cut into chunks

In bowl beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla.
Fold in toffee bits, serve with apples. Refrigerate any leftovers.
